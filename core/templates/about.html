Think of Zakat as "cleaning" your wealth. Here is how to fill out your fields accurately.

1. Cash on Hand & Bank

What it is: Every single Rupee or Dollar you have right now.
Where to find it: Physical cash in your wallet, money under your mattress, and balances in your banking apps.

2. Inventory (Market Value)

What it is: Items bought to sell for profit.
Important: Use the Current Selling Price (Market Value), not the original cost.
Exclude: Shop furniture, vans, or equipment.

3. Net Receivables

What it is: "Your money" currently in someone else's pocket.
Examples: Money lent to friends or customers who have taken goods but haven't paid their bill yet.

4. Short-term Liabilities

What it is: "Money Out" itemsâ€”debts you must pay soon.
Examples: Unpaid electricity bills, shop rent, or money owed to suppliers for inventory.
